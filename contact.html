Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What is your typical turnaround time?

Our turnaround time depends on the complexity and length of the project. Short-form videos typically take 1-3 days, while longer projects may take 5-7 days. We also offer rush services for urgent needs.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, we offer up to 3 rounds of revisions with our standard packages to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions can be arranged at a nominal fee.

How do I share my footage with you?

You can share your footage with us through cloud storage services like Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. For larger projects, we can also arrange alternative file transfer methods.

What are your payment terms?

We typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For long-term collaborations, we can discuss monthly retainer options.

Do you provide raw footage after editing?

We return all original footage provided to us. Project files can also be provided for an additional fee, depending on the complexity of the project.
